Help & Support

If you need assistance with SHID Alerts, here are some tips:

  1. Ensure your system is powered correctly and connected to your network.
  2. Use the “ARM” and “DISARM” buttons to control your system safely.
  3. Check the EEPROM logs to view past events.
  4. For technical issues, contact our support team.
